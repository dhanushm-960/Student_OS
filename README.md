# StudentOS

### Your AI-Powered Operating System for Student Success

StudentOS is an AI-powered student productivity and career development platform designed to help students manage academics, tasks, projects, skills, and placement preparation through a unified digital workspace.

The platform brings together planning, productivity, learning, project management, and career readiness into a single ecosystem, enabling students to stay organized, track their progress, and achieve their academic and professional goals efficiently.

---

## Problem Statement

Students often rely on multiple disconnected applications to manage academics, assignments, projects, learning resources, and placement preparation. This fragmented approach leads to poor organization, reduced productivity, and difficulty tracking long-term growth.

StudentOS addresses this challenge by providing a centralized platform that supports the entire student journey from goal setting to career readiness.

---

## Solution

StudentOS provides an all-in-one platform that enables students to:

* Plan and organize academic activities
* Manage daily tasks and assignments
* Track projects and milestones
* Follow personalized skill development roadmaps
* Prepare for internships and placements
* Receive AI-powered guidance and recommendations
* Monitor progress through analytics and insights
